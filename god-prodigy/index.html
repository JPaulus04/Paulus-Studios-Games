In-App Purchases

Optional Purchases

God Prodigy includes optional in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store payment system. Purchases are not required to play.

Death Shield
Consumable. Revives the player safely after defeat and prevents resource loss.
God's Mercy
Consumable. Defeats the next undefeated Elemental God boss and grants the related progression reward.
God Pass
Non-consumable. A one-time unlock that provides God Pass benefits such as +25% XP, bonus cosmetic support, and extra inventory space.

Restore Purchases

God Pass can be restored using the “Restore God Pass” option inside the in-game shop. Consumable purchases such as Death Shield and God's Mercy are not restored after use.

Troubleshooting

Common Support Items

Purchase Did Not Complete

  • Confirm you are signed into the App Store with a valid Apple ID.
  • Check that in-app purchases are allowed under Screen Time or Family Sharing settings.
  • Close and reopen the game, then try again.
  • For God Pass, use “Restore God Pass” if you already purchased it.

Game Progress

God Prodigy stores progress locally on your device. Uninstalling the game may delete local save data. Paulus Studios does not operate a server that stores or restores individual save files.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

God Prodigy does not require an account and does not collect personal information directly from players. Purchases are handled by Apple, and purchase status may be managed through RevenueCat.
