1. Introduction

Paulus Studios ("we," "our," or "us") operates God Prodigy, an iOS action RPG. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you play God Prodigy, how it is used, and what choices you have.

God Prodigy does not require an account or sign-in. We do not operate a player account server and do not collect personal information directly from players.

Short version: Paulus Studios does not collect, store, or sell your personal information. Game progress is stored locally on your device. In-app purchases are processed through Apple and purchase status may be managed through RevenueCat.

2. Data We Collect

Paulus Studios collects no personal data directly from players of God Prodigy.

There is no:

  • Account registration or login
  • Name, email address, or contact information collected by us inside the app
  • Location data collected by us
  • Advertising identifiers used by us
  • In-game chat or user-generated content

Apple may collect diagnostic, crash, purchase, and App Store data as part of iOS and the App Store framework. This is governed by Apple's privacy policies and settings.

3. Local Save Data

God Prodigy saves game progress using local device storage. This can include character level, inventory, resources, Stronghold upgrades, boss progress, and prestige status.

  • Save data is stored on your device
  • Save data is not transmitted to a Paulus Studios server
  • Save data may be included in an iCloud or device backup under Apple's settings
  • Uninstalling the game may delete local save data
Device-only storage No account required No server sync

4. Third-Party Services

God Prodigy uses third-party services for in-app purchases and purchase verification. We do not receive your payment information.

Apple In-App Purchases

Optional purchases in God Prodigy are processed by Apple's App Store payment infrastructure. Current in-app purchases include Death Shield, God's Mercy, and God Pass.

Paulus Studios does not see, receive, or store your payment card information, billing details, or Apple ID.

Apple Privacy Policy →

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to help manage in-app purchase status, such as whether God Pass is active. RevenueCat may collect limited technical and purchase-related information needed to verify purchase state.

We do not receive your payment details through RevenueCat.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy →

Advertising and Analytics

God Prodigy contains no third-party advertising SDKs. We do not use behavioral advertising or sell player data.

5. Children's Privacy

God Prodigy does not require account creation and Paulus Studios does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

  • No name, email, or contact information is requested inside the game
  • No account creation is required
  • In-app purchases are controlled through Apple's App Store authentication and parental controls

Parents and guardians can use Apple's Screen Time and Family Sharing features to manage in-app purchase permissions.

6. Your Rights

Because Paulus Studios does not collect personal data directly from players, there is no player account data stored by us to access, correct, export, or delete.

For data held by Apple or RevenueCat, contact those companies directly using the links in Section 4.

To delete local game data, uninstall God Prodigy from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
