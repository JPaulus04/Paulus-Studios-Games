Frequently Asked Questions

LSC How do I restore purchases after reinstalling Last Stand Command? ›
Open Last Stand Command and navigate to Settings → Store. Tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. If items don't appear, force-close the app, reopen, and try again.
LSC The game crashes on launch — what should I do? ›
Make sure you're running the latest App Store version. Fully close and relaunch the app. If it persists, restart your device. If crashes continue, contact us with your device model and iOS version so we can investigate.
LSC I was charged but didn't receive my in-app purchase. ›
First try Restore Purchases in Settings → Store. All transactions are processed by Apple. If the issue persists, visit reportaproblem.apple.com with your order ID. You can also contact us directly and we'll help resolve it.
LSC My progress was lost. Can it be recovered? ›
If iCloud sync was enabled, your progress may be stored — sign in with the same Apple ID and re-enable iCloud in game settings. Progress stored only locally may not be recoverable after an uninstall. We strongly recommend enabling iCloud backup.
GP When does God Prodigy release? ›
God Prodigy is currently in final development and approaching App Store submission. Release is coming soon. Follow this page for updates — the game card above will update when it's live.
GP What is the prestige system in God Prodigy? ›
After defeating all 10 Elemental Gods, you can Prestige — resetting your run but choosing a new class: Warrior (DEF + HP), Mage (XP + DEF pierce), Assassin (speed + crit), or God (all bonuses stacked). Your Legacy Armory weapons carry over permanently across every prestige.
GP What are Godkiller weapons? ›
Godkiller weapons are god-tier relics forged from dungeon fragments in the Legacy Armory. Three exist: Soulbreaker (15% lifesteal on every hit), Voidpiercer (ignores all enemy DEF), and Godsplitter (AoE stun every swing). Once unlocked, they stay in your Legacy Armory across all future playthroughs.
LSC GP Does Paulus Games collect personal data? ›
We collect only minimal anonymous data needed to operate our games (crash reports, anonymous session data). We do not sell your data to third parties. See the Privacy section below for full details on each game.

Privacy Policy

Last Stand Command — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Information We Collect

Last Stand Command collects anonymous gameplay data (level progress, session length) and device information (model, OS version) solely for improving the game and diagnosing crashes. We do not collect names, email addresses, or personally identifiable information through the game.

In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

We may use Apple's GameCenter and iCloud services for leaderboards and cloud saves. Their respective privacy policies apply to those services.

Data Retention & Deletion

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 12 months. You may request deletion of any data by contacting us via the support form.

Children's Privacy

This game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, please use the support form on this page.

God Prodigy — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Information We Collect

God Prodigy stores game progress locally on your device using your device's local storage. No personal information is transmitted to our servers. Anonymous crash diagnostics may be collected via Apple's standard mechanisms.

In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not store or have access to your payment information. IAP billing is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework.

Local Storage

Game save data (progress, inventory, settings) is stored locally on your device. This data does not leave your device and is not accessible to us. Uninstalling the app removes all local save data.

Data Retention & Deletion

We do not retain personal data on our servers. Local game data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app. For any privacy questions, contact us via the support form.

Children's Privacy

This game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, please use the support form.
